    Campus Facilities 

   Library : Centrally air-conditioned library housing over 1.25 lakh books, 2.5 lakh online books, 200 hardbound scientific journals, and access to approximately 2,000 online national and international journals.
   Laboratories : Well-equipped labs for various science disciplines.
   Computer Centers : State-of-the-art computer labs with high-speed internet.
   Auditorium : Fully equipped auditorium for seminars and events.
   Language Lab : Dedicated lab to enhance language skills.
   Hostels : Separate accommodations for male and female students with amenities like nutritious food, reading rooms, and sports facilities.
   Cafeteria : On-campus cafeteria offering a variety of hygienic food options.
   Sports Facilities : Facilities for basketball, volleyball, cricket, football, table tennis, and an indoor gymnasium.
   Wi-Fi Campus : High-speed internet connectivity throughout the campus.
   Medical Facilities : On-campus medical center for health emergencies.([Shiksha][6], [Careers360][7])

    Admissions 

   Undergraduate Programs : Admissions are merit-based, considering marks obtained in the 10+2 level examination or PUC (Pre-university course).
   Postgraduate Programs : Admissions are entrance-based, accepting scores from exams like KMAT and PGCET.
   Application Mode : Online
   Admission Cycle : Aligns with Bangalore University's schedule; specific dates may vary annually.
